How Claiming Works
Understanding the TikTok Goody Bag claim process
1

Login to TikTok Web

Go to tiktok.com in your browser, log in to your account.

2

Extract Your Cookies

Use a browser extension like Cookie-Editor or EditThisCookie to export your TikTok cookies. You need: msToken, sessionid, csrfToken, sid_guard, uid_tt and others.

3

Paste Cookies in Settings

Go to ⚙️ Settings and paste your cookies in key: value format (one per line) or semicolon-separated.

4

Fetch Goody Bag & Claim

Get the Room ID, fetch goody bag details, then use Claim Now or Auto Claim which fires 10 seconds before the goody bag opens.

⚠️ Note: Claiming requires valid TikTok session cookies. Cookies expire regularly and you'll need to refresh them. Use at your own risk and respect TikTok's rate limits.

Complete Guide
1

Find a TikTok LIVE with Goody Bag

Open TikTok app and find a streamer who is live and has set up a Goody Bag (you'll see a gift icon on their live). Note their username.

2

Fetch Room ID

Enter the TikTok username in the Fetch Room ID section and click Fetch. The Room ID will appear automatically.

3

Get Goody Bag Details

The Room ID auto-fills in the next section. Click Get Goody Bag Details to see: total coins, number of winners, countdown timer, participants, and the participation method (character to type).

4

Read Character Analysis

The Character Analysis shows you the exact Unicode characters needed to participate. Some goody bags require typing special emoji/Unicode characters in chat. Use Copy button to copy the exact character sequence.

5

Participate in the LIVE

Go to the TikTok LIVE, wait for the goody bag countdown, then type the participation character in chat at the right time.

6

(Optional) Auto Claim via API

For automated claiming, add your TikTok cookies in ⚙️ Settings. The Auto Claim feature will automatically send the participation request 10 seconds before the goody bag opens.
